Video 4

Around the time of making this video, the controversy of AI taking over various jobs and overall replacing artists was widely discussed online. Many artists spoke about the dangers of AI and how it may ruin thousands of people's lives due to certian art jobs becoming increasingly difficult ever since companies have started using AI art instead of hiring real, talented artists. So I wanted to make a video inspired by this controversy, how someone's job can easily be taken away and replaced with a machine with no soul.
