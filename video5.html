"Audio Test"

Created in 2023

While exploring different types of video making, I decided to make a video where the focal point of the video is the audio itself, not the visual aspect of the video. So what I did was I made a video that focuses on various sounds in order to make an interesting audio experience by the user imagining the visuals that would go along with the audio. Once I made the video, I extracted the audio as an mp3 audio file, seperating the video and the audio. To listen to the audio, click on the play button. Headphones are recommended.


After listening to the audio, click here to watch the original video!.
