"Audio Test"

Created in 2023


In order to continue to explore different types of video making, I decided to make a video where the focal point of the video is the audio itself, not visual aspect of the video. So what I did is I made the original video, and extracted the audio as an mp3 audio file. To listen to the audio, click on the play button to listen to the audio. Headphones are recommended. After listening to the audio, click here to watch the original video!.
