Daily Loop

Created in 2023

Fo this video, I challenged myself to create a video where it starts and ends as a loop. Something that I thought about that sort of acts as a loop is my everyday routine. Waking up, eating, doing activities, and sleeping is what everyone experiences. So I wanted to document my "version" of everyday life, and how my life during this time was sort of uninteresting.
