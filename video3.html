Daily Loop

Created in 2023


The challenge for this video was to make the video to start and end as a loop. Something that I thought about that sort of acts as a loop is living everyday. Waking up, eating, doing activities, and sleeping is what everyone experiences. So I wanted to document my "version" of daily life, and how my life during this time was sort of uninteresting.
