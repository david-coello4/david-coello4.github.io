Familiarity

Created in 2023


For this short video, the theme around it was nostalgia, so I wanted to make a short video based on the feeling of Deja Vu and how a picture, setting, and even music can trigger that feeling. So I decided to mash up a bunch of images that give of a Deja Vu feeling. Additionally, I used the music that the cat is playing through the instrument to add to that Deja Vu experience.
