DIY Bikini Bottom Photoshop

Created in 2024

I created my own DIY Photoshop with the theme of the ocean, using p5js coding. Using the numbers 0-9, you get different brushes or sea decorations to create your own scenery. Use x to reset and use p to take a screenshot of your creation!
