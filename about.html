About Me

David Coello is a digital artist who enjoys experimenting with all types of art. From illustration to coding, he expresses his creativity through storytelling, color, and implementing small elements of his personal life to his art pieces. His main work consists of 2D concept art, illustration, and animation. Currently, he is learning even more mediums of art such as 3D sculpting, but one day dreams of making his video game.
