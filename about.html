Artist Statement

My name is David Coello. I am a Mexican-American artist who lives and grew up in California, USA. From my neighborhood to experiences, every artwork that I create relates to my life and who I am. From illustration, character designing, animation, and web creating, I use a variety of softwares, mainly ProCreate as my main drawing program, and using HTML coding to enhance my artworks.

My inspiration of becoming an artist comes from growing up with video games. Playing on a GameBoy, Nintendo DS, Playstation 1; all of these nourished my creativity as a young kid and itâ€™s what inspired me to create art. Even to this day, I revisit some old favorites not just to relive my childhood memories, but to gain inspiration for what artworks I would like to create. My main work consists of character designing with a mix of storytelling. Many of the character designs I make all revolve around a story. Other than character designing, I work with HTML coding and have dabbled with P5js code to enhance my learning with these programs. Once I understand them thoroughly, I plan to learn other programs such as Python in order to start creating bigger projects.

All of these elements are what I love. Sure, some things may be difficult or frustrating as an artist, such as art blocks or codes not working, but that is what makes me grow as an artist. One day in the distant future, once I have much more experience, I plan to create my ultimate project. Making a Video Game.
