Find the Sea Creatures

Created in 2024

Using P5JS code and assets from "DIY Bikini Bottom Photoshop", I created a game where the goal is to locate each sea creature as they get smaller by each level.

To start, hover your mouse over the small crab in the center. Once done, it will randomly relocate and shrink in size. Pass all 4 levels fo win! Good Luck!
