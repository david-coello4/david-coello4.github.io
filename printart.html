3D Print Art

After processing the files, I first created a draft print, the image with a blank SpongeBob figure, to see how it printed in order to see if it worked out or it needs any changes. Once I felt confident about the structure of it, I finally printed out the entire build. Unfortunately, the SpongeBob that's part of the Patrick build broke off because of the weak supports, so I had to use the first drafted SpongeBob as a replacement. After that, I painted each with their appropriate colors and finished this project, as seen in the second colored image.
